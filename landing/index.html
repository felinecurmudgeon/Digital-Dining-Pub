Order and Pay from Your Smartphone

Digital Dining enables a new way to dine at your favorite restaurants.
Instant ordering from rich menu descriptions, one-click checkout from your smartphone and painless splitting of the bill with friends.

Manage Your Establishment More Efficiently

Say goodbye to skipped tabs, anxious customers and frenzied servers. Digital Dining gives restaurant and bar owners a streamlined way to manage reservations, fulfill kitchen orders and make bill splitting easy.

Modern Technology

Digital Dining is a full-stack Javascript application that uses using Ionic, AngularJS, Node/Express and mySQL. The inital application is over 11,000 lines of code and is available on GitHub. The prototype is hosted on Amazon Web Services.
